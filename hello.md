i - insert
esc - escape

h - left navigation
H - top of screen
j - down navigation
k - up navigation
l - right navigation
L - bottom of screen
M - middle of screen

i - editing in line before 
I - editing beginning of line 
a - editing in line after
A - editing end of line

x - delete letter
r - replace letter
R - replace word
dd - delete line

u - undo

/ - search for a word
n - search down
N - search up
